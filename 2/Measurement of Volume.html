📖 1. The Paper vs Book Secret

A single paper is 2D (Area). It has no room inside.

But when you stack papers, you create **HEIGHT**. Height gives an object "Room" to occupy space. This is Volume!

एउटा कागजको उचाइ हुँदैन, तर धेरै कागज मिल्दा उचाइ बन्छ र त्यसले ठाउँ ओगट्छ।

🧪 2. Measurement Methods (नाप्ने तरिकाहरू)

For objects like boxes or books, we use a ruler and a formula!

The Formula:

V = L × B × H

Volume = Length × Width × Height

How to measure a stone? It has no straight sides! We use water displacement.
