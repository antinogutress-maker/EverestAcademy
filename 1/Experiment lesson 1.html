🧪 Will It Dissolve?

Experiment Lesson

🎯 Why do we need this? (हामीलाई यो किन चाहिन्छ?)

Why:

Some questions can't be answered just by looking. We have to actually test them with an experiment (प्रयोग) to see what happens!

केही प्रश्नहरूको उत्तर हेरेर मात्र दिन सकिँदैन। के हुन्छ भनेर हेर्न हामीले वास्तवमै परीक्षण गर्नुपर्छ!

Where it is used:

When making sweet tea (knowing sugar dissolves in water) or when washing oily hands (knowing water alone won't wash away oil).

गुलियो चिया बनाउँदा (चिनी पानीमा घुल्छ भनेर थाहा पाउन) वा चिल्लो हात धुँदा (पानीले मात्र चिल्लो जाँदैन भनेर बुझ्न)।

The Solubility Experiment

💧 Water (पानी)
🛢️ Cooking Oil (तेल)

🎉 Conclusion (निष्कर्ष)

"You did it! You discovered that salt dissolves completely in water, but it just sinks in the oil!"

"तपाईंले गर्नुभयो! तपाईंले पत्ता लगाउनुभयो कि नुन पानीमा पूर्ण रूपमा घुल्छ, तर तेलमा यो तल बस्छ!"

When we can't find an answer just by looking, we do an experiment to uncover the secret!

जब हामी हेरेर मात्र उत्तर पाउन सक्दैनौं, हामी रहस्य पत्ता लगाउन एउटा प्रयोग (Experiment) गर्छौं!

📚 Important Words to Know

Experiment
प्रयोग (Prayog)

A hands-on test we do in real life to see if our ideas are correct or wrong.

Inquiry
खोजतलास (Khojtalas)

Asking curious questions and actively searching for the answers.

Solubility
घुलनशीलता (Ghulanshilta)

The ability of a substance (like salt or sugar) to melt or 'dissolve' into a liquid.
