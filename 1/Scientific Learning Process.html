Drop Here!
Step 1: Observe (अवलोकन)

Look at the flashlight. Click the red switch.

👁️ Nothing happened! It is broken.
Step 2: Ask (प्रश्न)

Now that we observed it is broken, what should we do?

❓ Why isn't it turning on?
Step 3: Guess (अनुमान)
Step 4: Test (परीक्षण)

Drag the batteries near the glowing green box!

Step 5: Conclusion (निष्कर्ष)

Your guess was right! ✅ You solved it!

📚 5. Key Words to Remember

👁️

Observe (अवलोकन)

Looking closely at something.

(ध्यान दिएर हेर्नु)

💡

Hypothesis (परिकल्पना)

A smart guess before testing.

(परीक्षण अघिको बाठो अनुमान)

🧪

Experiment (प्रयोग)

Testing if your guess is right.

(आफ्नो अनुमान सही छ कि छैन जाँच्नु)

✅

Conclusion (निष्कर्ष)

The final answer you found out.

(तपाईंले पत्ता लगाएको अन्तिम उत्तर)
